Credential Technologies Pvt. Ltd. was started in the year 2003 with an aim of manufacturing higher end PU Leathers from Wet Blue Splits and lower end grain hides.

br> Using the state of art imported machinery the desired quality output is manufactured. The capacity of the unit is 300,000 to 400,000 sq. feet per month. At present the unit caters to the need of raw material suppliers (Leather Suppliers) to all the leading leather goods exporters of the country.

It also exports finished leathers to China, Italy and United Kingdom.

Our products

Polyurethane finished leather has a fine film of textured Polyurethane on the top and specially crusted split or lowend grain hides at the bottom. It has the grain look, feel, flex, breathablity and drape of the top grain leather. In addition, it has better abrasion resistance and water resistance characteristics.
